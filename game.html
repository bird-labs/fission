How To Play!
Click once to put an Atom
Click again on your turn to do a fission!
Yet again, for a triplet
Inner atoms explode at 4, edge atoms explode at 3 while corner atoms explode at 2 fissions. On explosion the neighbouring atoms will be fused with the new atoms and convert to that color.

The game terminates when the entire board has just one color of atoms.
